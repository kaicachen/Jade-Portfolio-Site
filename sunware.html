Project Overview

Design a furniture set for a market/ concept and explore unique connection details.

8 Weeks

Partner Project

Concept

Inspired by the autumn ritual of drying persimmons, the set should celebrate natural materials, the vibrant hue of ripe fruit, and handcrafted details

Ceramics is a natural material with unique properties to incorporate into the design for a light, warm, and sensory experience.

CONTEXT

MARKET

The set is targeted towards high-end residential clients, architects, and interior designers.

How can we create an indoor/outdoor furniture set that embodies the ritual of drying persimmons and leverage the colorful, moldable, and compressive properties of ceramics?

SETTLE BENCH

With ceramic ends and a slightly curved profile, Settle gently draws people together. Designed for gathering and grounding, Settle offers a warm moment of respite.

SWAY SCREEN

A partition that glows in the sun, Sway softens and envelops a space without closing it off. It references the quiet swaying of hanging persimmons drying in the autumn air.
