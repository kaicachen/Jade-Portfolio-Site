Project Overview

Duration: 6 Weeks
Group Size: Individual Project
Date Created: Spring 2025

Research

Hydroponics are a great option to bring purpose and nature into senior living homes. Hydroponic planters are also utilized for horticulture therapy programs, especially when full gardens aren’t feasible. Most hydroponic systems on the market are not adapted for older users and have many ergonomic issues.

User: Lin

Bio:
• 76 Years Old | Female
• Living in assisted senior living
• $: $-$$

Lin loves gardening but has trouble with traditional gardening now as it requires too much bending down and digging. She needs help sometimes with various tasks and now lives in assisted living.

Pain Points:
• Small fine motor actions
• Bending and reaching
• Tech heavy interfaces

Wants + Needs:
• Simple gardening activity without too much physical strain
• Small scale personal product
