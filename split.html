FOOD TRUCKS

Food trucks often set out condiments, napkins, straws and more on the counter of their truck. This area is small and often crowded so organization is key.

Food trucks often have bright colors and unique aesthetics to draw attention. Many salt and pepper grinder designs on the market do not cater to this look.

How can we create a ___ salt and pepper grinder set for food trucks that stores and sets up well with the food truck and promotes customers to return them to their proper place?
